JIRA ==> one of the most commonly used for Software Development teams for management 
Benefits: 
    1. Issue Tracking --> Epic, User Story, Task/SubTask, Defect/Bug, Test Cases 
    2. Bug Tracking 
    3. Agile Boards --> Scrum and Kanban 
    4. Backlogs --> Product Backlog, Sprint Backlog
    5. Graphics 

PO/SM and Upper Management is responsible for creating JIRA projects + creating Boards + adding Epics/User Stories + 
providing access to development team members.
